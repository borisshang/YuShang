Diabetes Prediction for LifeStory Health, Inc.

Sep 2022 - Dec 2022

  • A python-based vision screening tool to predict diabetes among patients for LifeStory Health, Inc.
  • Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the raw data using Python and Excel to analyze and visualize the data, gaining valuable insights into its characteristics.
  • Performed correlation analysis to establish the relationship between various diseases and diabetes, which helped identify significant factors contributing to diabetes.
  • Developed predictive models for diabetes using a variety of machine learning algorithms, including Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, KNN, and Random Forest, to estimate the likelihood of diabetes based on patient data.
  • Evaluated model performance using the root mean square error (RMSE) metric, fine-tuning and enhancing the models to improve their accuracy and reliability for diabetes prediction.
